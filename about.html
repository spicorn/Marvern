Learn About Our Mineral Mavern Mining

1Foreign investment agency

We are the one stop shop for foreign investors who need to acquire suitable mining claims for outright purchase or joint venture. We paid our clients with various mine owners in different mining sectors such as Gold, Lithium, Chrome, Antimony, Mica and various other minerals. We further offer various services necessary support for foreign investors to set up solid operations in Zimbabwe.This services include:

  • Company registration
  • Permit and Licence acquisition
  • Partnership Investment agreements
  • Off-take agreements

2Regulatory compliance

We assist mining companies in understanding and complying with local, national, and international laws and regulations governing mining activities.

3Permitting and licensing

We help our valued clients (both locals and foreigners) navigate the complex permitting and licensing processes required for mining operations, including environmental permits and mineral rights.

4Contract drafting and negotiation

We guide our client through the complex area of by drafting and reviewing contracts related to mining activities, such as joint venture agreements, mineral leases, and offtake agreements, to protect clients' interests.

5Dispute resolution

We help our clients to avoid and resolve disputes through negotiation, mediation, arbitration, or litigation related to mining contracts, property rights, environmental issues, and other legal matters.

6Environmental compliance

We advise our valued clients on environmental regulations and as well as assist on the development strategies to minimize environmental impact, manage waste, and comply with reclamation requirements. We prepare environmental impact assessment reports.

7Land use and property rights

We assist our valued clients in resolving issues related to land ownership, property rights, access rights, and easements for mining activities.

8Health and safety compliance

We ensure that mining companies meet health and safety standards to protect workers and comply with occupational health and safety laws.

9Corporate governance

We advise on corporate governance issues, compliance with securities laws, and best practices for corporate structuring and governance in the mining sector.

10Mergers and acquisitions

We are experts in due diligence processes, negotiating transaction agreements, and navigating legal issues related to mergers, acquisitions, and divestitures in the mining industry.

11 Stakeholder engagement and community relations

It is important for mining companies to establish positive relationships with local communities, Indigenous groups, government authorities, and other stakeholders to secure social license to operate and address concerns effectively. We offer stakeholder engagement services by bridging the gap between mining companies and indigenous groups.
